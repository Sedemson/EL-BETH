About Us

Message From Our HeadMistress

Welcome to the official website of EL-BETH ACADEMY, where you can discover what makes our school a special place for learning, growth, and development. At EL-BETH ACADEMY, we are a family-oriented school dedicated to nurturing confident, disciplined, and academically sound students. We follow the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum, offering a strong foundation from Pre-school through to Junior High School (JHS). Our caring and professional staff provide a safe and stimulating environment where every child is valued and supported to reach their full potential. We are committed to academic excellence, moral upbringing, and the holistic development of each learner. Whether in the classroom, on the playground, or during special school events, EL-BETH ACADEMY promotes a culture of high expectations and positive values. We warmly invite prospective parents to visit our campus and witness the EL-BETH ACADEMY experience — a vibrant and nurturing community where future leaders are shaped.
